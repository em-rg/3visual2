European Banks and Government Debt

The visualization compares the sovereign debt exposure of major European banks to the public debt ratio (as % of GDP) of their countries of origin. Each bank is represented according to the country of origin, and from its balance capacity, based on the part of financing that comes from its foundation. The size and opacity of dots represents the flow and quality of debt investment in the various states is displayed for each bank. The positioning of banks, according to the relationship between public debt (% of GDP) and from the exposure of sovereign, based on the proportion of bank foundations.

Source: The Guardian, Financial Data

How to read it?

Banks size represents bank's financial foundation (in billions)
X-axis: Country's Public Debt (% of GDP)
Y-axis: Bank's Sovereign Exposure (in billions)
Color: Country/Region
